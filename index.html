About us


Our goal at the Systems Biology of Host-Microbiome Interactions group is to study gut microbiome functions and their regulation to better understand the interactions with the host in health and disease.

To achieve our goals, we use a Systems Biology approach that combines experimental work, using in vitro and in vivo models of the gut microbial communities, with multiomic profiling and computational data analysis and integration.
